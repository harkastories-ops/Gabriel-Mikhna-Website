About Gabriel

Gabriel Mikhna is an independent producer and songwriter crafting a distinctive sound in modern indie pop — blending folk warmth, retro love songs, chill grooves, emotional ballads, and uplifting mid-tempo tracks.

His music spans a wide emotional range — from warmth and nostalgia to vulnerability, ambition, and personal growth.
